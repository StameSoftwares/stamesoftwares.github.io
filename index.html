What's New

NeatNotes v1.4.0

The biggest update yet. More ways to organize, create, and stay focused.

NEW

Vertical Page Layout Mode

Toggle the infinite canvas into a structured physical notebook. A4 pages with a premium desk background, drop shadows, and intelligent auto-pagination. Pages are added automatically as you draw and trimmed when you erase. Manual additions are fully tracked in Undo/Redo.

High-Performance PDF Engine

PDFs are now lazy-loaded from isolated storage instead of being crammed into Base64 strings. No more out-of-memory crashes on large textbooks.

Handwriting to Text

Convert handwritten notes to three beautiful typed fonts. Multi-line formatting preserved perfectly.

NEW

Layering Controls

Bring to Front & Send to Back controls in the Smart Menu to arrange strokes and shapes.

NEW

Barrel Button Eraser

Better Math Graphs

Critical Bug Fixes FIXES
Canvas Pointer Lock

Using keyboard shortcuts mid-draw can no longer permanently lock the canvas.

GPU Memory Crash

Resolved a hard crash in the Undo stack caused by shallow-copying GPU textures.

Sidebar Sync

New notebooks now immediately appear in the sidebar without requiring a restart.

Ultrawide Panning

Camera clamping math corrected. No more jitter on large or ultrawide monitors.

Core Features

Built for power users

Everything you need to capture ideas, solve problems, and organize your thoughts — natively on Windows.

Infinite Canvas

Pan, zoom, and expand your notes in any direction. No walls, no limits.

Vertical Page Layout

Switch to a structured, physical notebook feel with A4 pages, auto-pagination, and adaptive guidelines.

Native Performance

Ultra-low latency WinUI 3 inking with full pressure-sensitivity support for your stylus.

PDF Import

Import and annotate PDFs without memory crashes. Lazy-loading keeps everything blazing fast.

Smart Math Solver

Handwrite any equation, lasso it, and solve it instantly. Plot graphs right on the canvas.

Total Privacy

All files saved locally. No accounts, no cloud, no data harvesting — ever.

Toolset

Every tool you need.

A pen for every thought.

Choose from pressure-sensitive brushes including the Studio Pen, Monoline, Calligraphy, Pencil, Marker, and Highlighter. All with full stylus pressure support.

Precision editing.

Standard Eraser, Partial Eraser for pixel-perfect cleanup, Stroke Eraser to wipe entire lines instantly. Now shapes are protected from accidental erasure.

Structure your chaos.

Lines, arrows, rectangles, circles. Colorful sticky notes for quick reminders. Dynamic tables to organize data. Now with Z-index layering controls.

Calculations on the canvas.

Handwrite a complex math equation, lasso it, hit Solve. NeatNotes calculates the answer instantly using a powerful local math engine. Guided tutorial included.

Visualize your equations.

Plot equations as dynamic 2D graphs right alongside your handwritten notes. Now drag from anywhere in the graph and double-click center to edit the equation.
